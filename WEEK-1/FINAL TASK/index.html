ARCANE

Amid the growing turmoil between Piltover’s prosperous utopia and Zaun’s oppressed underbelly, sisters Vi and Powder become entangled in a rising conflict fueled by opposing ideals and the emergence of arcane technology.

Main Characters

Once an innocent and playful girl named Powder, Jinx is a volatile and unpredictable figure shaped by tragedy. Haunted by her past and desperate for validation, she embraces chaos, wielding devastating weapons and a manic persona. Her fractured psyche and yearning for acceptance drive her to acts of both brilliance and destruction, making her one of Zaun’s most feared figures.

JINX

A ruthless and calculating leader, Silco is the mastermind behind Zaun’s rebellion against Piltover. Once a close friend of Vander, he now seeks to establish Zaun’s independence at any cost. Though cruel and manipulative, he deeply cares for Jinx, treating her like a daughter. His vision for Zaun is built on power and fear, making him both an inspiring leader and a dangerous adversary.

SILCO

The former leader of Zaun’s rebellion and a father figure to Vi and Powder, Vander is a man of principle who has turned away from violence in favor of protecting his people through diplomacy. He believes in keeping the peace between Piltover and Zaun, even if it means making compromises. His protective nature and unwavering sense of duty make him a beloved figure in the undercity, but his past continues to haunt him.

Best Scene

Season 2 Ending Scene

After the hive mind collapses, Jinx clings to a beam while Vi and Warwick lie below. Vi hesitates over Warwick, her former father figure, before he suddenly attacks. Jinx knocks him off the beam but falls with him. Vi catches Jinx, but Warwick grabs her leg, forcing Jinx to make a choice. She pulls Vi’s gauntlet gemstone, slipping from Vi’s grip and detonating a bomb as she falls.

Episodes

Season 1 Episode 1 — Welcome to the Playground

Vi, Powder, and their friends Vander, Mylo, and Claggor venture into Piltover to steal from a wealthy scientist. Their heist goes wrong, leading to a citywide manhunt and escalating tensions between Piltover and Zaun. Meanwhile, Silco, a sinister figure from Vander’s past, begins enacting his plans.

Season 1 Episode 2 — Some Mysteries Are Better Left Unsolved

Jayce, a young scientist, is interrogated for experimenting with arcane magic. Viktor, a fellow scientist, offers to help him prove its potential. Meanwhile, Silco’s growing influence in Zaun threatens Vander’s efforts to keep the peace, forcing him into a difficult position.

Season 1 Episode 3 — The Base Violence Necessary for Change

LSilco launches an attack on Vander, leading to a devastating battle. Vi, Powder, and their friends attempt to save him, but Powder’s unstable use of magic-infused explosives results in tragedy. Vander dies protecting them, and a devastated Vi lashes out at Powder, who then falls into Silco’s hands.

Season 1 Episode 4 — Happy Progress Day!

Years later, Jayce and Viktor revolutionize Piltover with Hextech advancements. Meanwhile, a transformed Powder—now Jinx—wreaks havoc, proving her loyalty to Silco. Caitlyn, a determined enforcer, begins investigating Zaun’s underground dealings, leading her toward Vi, now imprisoned.

Season 1 Episode 5 — Everybody Wants to Be My Enemy

Caitlyn frees Vi to help track down Jinx. Meanwhile, Jinx struggles with her fractured mind and insecurities about her place in Silco’s plans. Tensions between Piltover and Zaun intensify as Silco tightens his grip on the Undercity.

Season 1 Episode 6 — When These Walls Come Tumbling Down

Vi and Caitlyn journey deeper into Zaun, uncovering the growing effects of Shimmer, Silco’s dangerous drug. Jinx’s instability worsens after an encounter with Vi, while Viktor takes a desperate step to save himself through Hexcore experimentation.
